Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ZenMaster Sudoku respects your privacy. This policy explains how data is handled when using the application.

Data Collection

ZenMaster Sudoku does not collect personal data that directly identifies users.

  • A player nickname may be requested and stored locally on the device for scores and rankings.
  • This nickname is not transmitted to our servers.
  • No user account is created.

Advertising

The application may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks.

  • These services may collect anonymized technical data (advertising identifier, device type, language).
  • ZenMaster Sudoku does not access personal data collected by these providers.

Analytics

ZenMaster Sudoku does not use internal analytics tools. Any data collection is performed solely by third-party advertising services.

Children’s Privacy

ZenMaster Sudoku is suitable for all audiences. No personal data is knowingly collected from children.

Data Security

Local data (nickname, progress) is stored only on your device. No personal data is stored on external servers.

Changes

This policy may be updated if the application evolves. Any changes will be published on this page.
